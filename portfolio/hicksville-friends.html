Friends at Hicksville

  • Date: August 2015

Playing around in Illustrator and Photoshop to caricature a photo of me and 2 friends and overlayed it on another photo. The image encapsulates the feeling of friendship and bonding, and our sadness about leaving each other again after our annual reunion trip to a magical place called Hicksville.
