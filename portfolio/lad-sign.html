life/after/denim custom signage

  • Client: life/after/denim
  • Date: July 2011

Hand-made made-to-order life/after/denim signage from aluminum sheet lettering nailed to sanded and varnished custom-cut wooden planks. Signage was both sent to retailers that carried life/after/denim to hang in their stores as part of the merchandising of the brand, as well as used for in-house merchandising at ENK trade shows.
