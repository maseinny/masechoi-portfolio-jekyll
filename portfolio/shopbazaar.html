ShopBAZAAR ecommerce platform

  • Client: ShopBAZAAR

Project manager for the site replatform, rebuild, and ongoing maintenance of Harper's Bazaar magazine's eCommerce arm, ShopBAZAAR. The site build for ShopBAZAAR entailed not only the front end on top of a powerful proprietary eCommerce CMS, but required we build a custom vendor management platform which improved the process by which all of ShopBAZAAR's partners uploaded content to their site, and view and fulfill orders placed on ShopBAZAAR.

I was responsible for getting the system built, finding solutions and helping to think through and streamline backend user and process flows, managing the development schedule, handling and integrating all third party APIs and integrations, and maintaining/updating/user-testing the site, as well as communicating with the client on a daily basis and conducting training meetings for the client team at Harper's Bazaar headquarters.
