When You Wish crowdfunding sponsor process

  • Client: When You Wish
  • Date: December 2012

Designed the sign in and registration pages for crowdfunding site, When You Wish. It was important for us to regularly evaluate the sign in page and registration process to ensure it was easy and intuitive for users since an unstreamlined or badly designed sign up process can be a big hindrance to new user acquisition.
